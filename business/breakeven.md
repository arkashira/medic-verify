# Breakeven Analysis
## Unit Economics
### Cost per Active User
The estimated cost per active user for the medic-verify platform is broken down into compute, storage, and bandwidth costs.

* Compute: $0.05 per user per month (assuming 10% utilization of a $5/month server)
* Storage: $0.01 per user per month (assuming 1GB storage per user at $0.01/GB-month)
* Bandwidth: $0.005 per user per month (assuming 100MB bandwidth per user at $0.05/GB)
* Total: $0.065 per user per month

## Pricing Tiers
The medic-verify platform will offer three pricing tiers:

1. **Basic**: $9.99/month (100 users, 1GB storage, 100MB bandwidth)
	* Features: Limited to 100 users, basic reporting, and community support
2. **Pro**: $49.99/month (1,000 users, 10GB storage, 1GB bandwidth)
	* Features: Includes all Basic features, plus advanced reporting, priority support, and single sign-on (SSO)
3. **Enterprise**: $199.99/month (10,000 users, 100GB storage, 10GB bandwidth)
	* Features: Includes all Pro features, plus customized onboarding, dedicated support, and advanced security features

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Range
The estimated CAC range for the medic-verify platform is between $50 and $200 per user, depending on the marketing channels and sales strategies employed.

## Lifetime Value (LTV) Estimate
The estimated LTV for a medic-verify user is $300, assuming an average customer lifespan of 6 months and an average revenue per user (ARPU) of $50.

## Break-even Analysis
To calculate the break-even point, we need to determine the number of users required to cover the costs of acquiring and serving those users.

* Average Revenue per User (ARPU): $50 (assuming 50% of users are on the Pro tier and 25% are on the Enterprise tier)
* Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $125 (midpoint of the estimated range)
* Lifetime Value (LTV): $300
* Break-even point: 250 users (assuming a 6-month customer lifespan and a $50 ARPU)

## Path to $10K MRR
To reach $10,000 in monthly recurring revenue (MRR), we would need:

* 200 users on the Pro tier ($49.99/month) and 50 users on the Enterprise tier ($199.99/month)
* Alternatively, 400 users on the Basic tier ($9.99/month) and 100 users on the Pro tier ($49.99/month)

This would require a combination of effective marketing, sales, and customer success strategies to acquire and retain users across the different pricing tiers.
