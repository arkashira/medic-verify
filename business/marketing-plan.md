## 6️⃣ Success Metrics (90‑Day Targets)  

| Metric | Target (by D +90) | Rationale |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | **≥ 220** (average across free‑trial + paid) | 30 % conversion from trial (≈ 70 trial users) + 150 paid users (5 % of total market outreach). |
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** | **$30,000** (≈ 25 × $1,200 / month plans) | Achieves 3‑month CAC payback at $250 CAC; validates product‑market fit. |
| **Lead‑to‑Trial Conversion** | **12 %** | From LinkedIn + webinar leads (≈ 1,000 leads → 120 trials). |
| **Trial‑to‑Paid Conversion** | **20 %** | Driven by audit‑free trial and strong compliance ROI messaging. |
| **Customer NPS** | **≥ 55** | Early‑adopter satisfaction to fuel referrals. |
| **Churn (first 90 days)** | **≤ 5 %** | Indicates product reliability meets expectations. |

*If any metric falls >10 % short of target, trigger a rapid‑iteration sprint (content refresh, CAC re‑allocation, or pricing tweak).*
